About Club

The Revolution Software Development Club at Vishwakarma University was deep-rooted on 20th June, 2020 with the idea to open up a separate workspace for the students to explore different technologies to get established and lay the grounds for the improvisation leading to tomorrow’s Industries. The club aims to improve the quality of learning and lead every individual to adapt to different work spaces. The organization keeps the track of every aspect of qualitative learning which helps the club to imbibe more and more insights on an individual’s overall development. The Revolution Software Development Club (RSDC) focuses more on practical approach rather than focusing on the theoretical parts of certain modules. An individual will be tested upon how he/she can use their skill set to tackle a particular problem and provide an optimized solution, being the ultimate goal as to make the most effective use of the situations or the provided resources.
